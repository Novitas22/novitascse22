NOVITAS'22

It is an National level Technical symposium organized by the Dhirajlal Gandhi college of Technology from the department of computer science and Engineering. This events includes many event like paper presentation, project presentation/Expo and other events. Novitas'22 invites the young intelligent minds of various colleges and sets forth tremendous means to exhibit their valuable ideas. It is our sincere effort to provide a healthy platform for the students to interact and exchange their ideas on recent technical advancements and to exhibit their hidden talents.
