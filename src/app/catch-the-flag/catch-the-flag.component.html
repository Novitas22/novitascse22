Capture the Flag

This is a jeopardy style capture the flag event where cyber security enthusiasts solve labyrinths in order to score points. The competitors are pitted against each other in a quest to capture the 'flag', whilst demonstrating their expertise in OSINT, Cryptanalysis, Reverse Engineering and various other Cyber security domains.
