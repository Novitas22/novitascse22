Capture the Flag

Capture the Flag is a classic team game played indoors or outdoors. Two teams each have their own color flag placed in their “home base.” The objective is to steal the other team’s flag and take it to their own base.

• Maximum 3 Members Allowed

• Individual Participants can also Participate
